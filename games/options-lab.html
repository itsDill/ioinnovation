Long Call

A long call gives you the right to buy stock at the strike price. You profit when the stock rises above the strike plus premium paid.

  • Unlimited profit potential as stock rises
  • Limited risk: maximum loss is the premium paid
  • Best used when you're bullish and expect significant upside
  • Benefits from increased volatility
