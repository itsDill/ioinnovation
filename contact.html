Frequently Asked Questions

Are your tools free to use?

Yes! All tools on IO Web Tools are completely free to use. No registration, no hidden fees, and no limits on usage. We believe in providing accessible tools for everyone.

Do you store my data when using the tools?

No, we don't store any of your data. All calculations and processing happen locally in your browser. Your information never leaves your device, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Can I suggest new tools or features?

Absolutely! We love hearing from our users. You can suggest new tools or features by opening an issue on our GitHub repository or joining our Discord community to discuss your ideas.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

The best way to report bugs is through our GitHub Issues page. Please include details about what you were doing, what browser you're using, and any error messages you saw.

Can I use these tools for commercial purposes?

Yes, you're free to use our tools for both personal and commercial purposes. There are no restrictions on how you use the calculations or results from our tools.

Do you have mobile apps?

Not yet, but our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. You can access all tools directly from your mobile browser. Mobile apps may be considered in the future based on user demand.
