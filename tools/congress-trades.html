Frequently Asked Questions

How do I see Nancy Pelosi's stock trades?
Use our free Congress Stock Tracker above to see Nancy Pelosi's latest stock purchases and sales. All data comes from official congressional financial disclosures filed with the House of Representatives under the STOCK Act.
Are congressional stock trades public?
Yes! Under the STOCK Act of 2012, members of Congress must publicly disclose stock trades within 45 days. Our tracker compiles this data automatically so you can see what politicians are buying and selling.
Should I copy congressional stock trades?
While you can see what politicians buy, be aware there's a 45-day disclosure delay. Some politicians may have information advantages through committee memberships. Use this data for research and idea generation, not as direct trading signals. Always do your own due diligence.
Which politicians make the most stock trades?
The most active congressional traders in recent years include Nancy Pelosi (D-CA), Tommy Tuberville (R-AL), Dan Crenshaw (R-TX), Josh Gottheimer (D-NJ), and several others. Our tracker ranks politicians by trading volume and shows their recent stock picks.
Where does this data come from?
All data comes from official financial disclosures filed with the Clerk of the House of Representatives and the Secretary of the Senate. These are public records required by the STOCK Act and Ethics in Government Act.
