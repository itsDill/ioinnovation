About 13F Filings

What is a 13F?

A 13F is a quarterly report filed with the SEC by institutional investment managers with over $100 million in assets under management, disclosing their equity holdings.

Who Files?

Major hedge funds, pension funds, asset managers, and institutional investors. Following these "smart money" moves can provide valuable investment insights and trend identification.
