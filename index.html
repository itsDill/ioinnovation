Frequently Asked Questions

What is a 13F filing and how can I use the visualizer?

A 13F filing is a quarterly SEC report that institutional investment managers with over $100 million in assets must file. Our 13F Visualizer lets you explore holdings of hedge funds like Berkshire Hathaway, Bridgewater, and Renaissance Technologies to understand their investment strategies and track changes over time.

Are all IO Innovate tools really free?

Yes! All our financial tools, market analysis, and educational resources are 100% free. No account signup required, no hidden fees, no premium tiers. We're supported by non-intrusive advertising to keep everything accessible to everyone.

How often is the market data updated?

Our 13F data is updated quarterly as new filings become available (typically 45 days after quarter end). The Economic Calendar is updated daily with upcoming events, and 10K summaries are generated as companies file their annual reports with the SEC.

Can I use these tools for investment decisions?

Our tools provide data and analysis to help you research investments, but they should not be considered financial advice. Past performance doesn't guarantee future results. Always consult with a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.
