Yes! All tools on IO Web Tools are 100% free to use with no hidden fees, subscriptions, or limitations. We believe professional-grade tools should be accessible to everyone. Our platform is supported by ads and optional donations.

How secure are your tools? Do you store my data?

Privacy and security are our top priorities. Most of our tools process data client-side (in your browser), meaning your data never leaves your device. For tools that require server processing, data is immediately deleted after use and never stored permanently.

What categories of tools do you offer?

We offer 100+ tools across four main categories: Finance Tools (calculators, converters, investment tools), Technology Tools (developer utilities, formatters, generators), SEO & Marketing Tools (analyzers, keyword tools, social media tools), and Privacy & Security Tools (password generators, encryption, privacy checkers).

Can I use these tools for commercial purposes?

Absolutely! All our tools are free for both personal and commercial use. Whether you're a freelancer, business owner, developer, or enterprise, you can use our tools without any restrictions or licensing requirements.
